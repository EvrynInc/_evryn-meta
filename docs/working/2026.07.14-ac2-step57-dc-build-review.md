### F1 ‚Äî [Correctness / silent-failure] The 54b persisted-forward lookup is not origin-scoped
`verdict.ts persist54bForward` and `outcome.ts reattributeForwardOnMatch` both select the persisted forward by **`metadata->>emailmgr_item_id` ALONE** with `.limit(1)` and no `origin` filter.
